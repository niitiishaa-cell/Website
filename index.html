// hot takes (and i stand by them)

THE TAKES.

If you sound like everyone else, you're already a commodity. Tone is your only defensible moat before product-market fit — and most brands throw it in the trash for "professional."

Hero assets are a flex, not a strategy. Daily, systemic execution builds brands. Build the engine before you obsess over the paint job.

If your team can't answer "how does this brand speak?" in 10 seconds, you don't have a brand — you have vibes. Vibes don't scale. Systems do.

A brand deck is not a strategy. A strategy is a set of irreversible decisions about who you are, who you're not, and how that translates into behavior — every single day.
