Common questions

Things people ask before working with me.

Will LinkedIn ghostwriting actually sound like me? +
Yes. The whole point is that nobody should know I wrote it. Before I write a single word, I spend time understanding how you speak, what you believe, your hot takes, your stories. The result is content that sounds so much like you, you might forget you did not write it.
How long before I see results on LinkedIn? +
Realistically, 60 to 90 days for meaningful traction. LinkedIn rewards consistency. The first month is about laying foundations and finding what resonates. By month two and three, reach tends to accelerate noticeably. I do not promise overnight virality. I do promise a profile that builds real authority over time.
What does a D2C website audit include exactly? +
Everything. Homepage, product pages, collection pages, cart, checkout, mobile experience, copy, CTAs, trust signals, load speed, and funnel flow. You get a document that ranks every issue by its likely revenue impact and tells you exactly what to change and why.
Do you implement the website changes yourself? +
For Shopify and most D2C platforms, yes. I handle the copy rewrites and structural changes directly. For technical development needs, I work alongside your dev or recommend someone I trust.
Do you take on multiple clients at once? +
Intentionally limited. I take on a small number of clients at a time so every project gets the depth of attention it deserves. If you are reading this and spots are open, that is a good sign to reach out now.
