Marketing Foundations

Growth hacks do not fix broken plumbing. Before you scale spend, you need the unsexy infrastructure that actually captures demand.

The Leaky Bucket

Startups are obsessed with top-of-funnel traffic. They pour thousands of dollars into performance media while ignoring lifecycle, retention, and basic tracking.

Traffic lands on a broken page. Emails go to spam. There is no welcome series. The customer buys once and is forgotten forever.

You aren't acquiring customers; you are renting them from algorithms at a premium.

The Plumbing

I build the marketing operations that catch the traffic you pay for. Setting up the pipes so that every dollar spent is measurable, and every user acquired is retained.

  • 01. Lifecycle Architecture: Mapping the post-click journey. Setting up core email/SMS flows (Welcome, Abandonment, Win-back) before running a single ad.
  • 02. Data & Attribution: Establishing clear feedback loops. If we don't know exactly what action drove the conversion, the data is useless.
  • 03. Conversion Rate Optimization: Tweaking the product page narrative. Traffic is irrelevant if the landing environment doesn't convert.
