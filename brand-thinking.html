The Science of Linguistic Architecture.

We don't "write posts." We extract your mental models and convert them into a proprietary digital dialect that is impossible to replicate.

The greatest risk to a founder's personal brand is Inauthenticity. If your LinkedIn sounds like a marketing agency wrote it, you've already lost the trust of your peers, your investors, and your team.

My methodology ensures that every word published under your name matches your internal syntax—the specific way you think, pause, and project authority.

The Goal

"I want your mother to think you wrote it, but your competitors to think you hired a philosopher."

The Linguistic Audit

Before we go live, I perform a deep-dive analysis into your existing communication. This isn't about what you say, but how you say it.

Analysis A

Sentence Cadence

Do you use staccato fragments or long, analytical clauses? We map your natural rhythm to ensure the "voice" feels familiar to those who know you.

Analysis B

Lexical Selection

Every founder has "Anchor Words"—the vocabulary that defines their worldview. We identify these and build a "Forbidden List" of words you would never use.

The Extraction Framework

  • The Enemy Map

    Every great brand stands against something. We define your industry's status quo—the "enemy"—and sharpen your perspective against it. This creates immediate polarization and authority.

  • Intellectual DNA Extraction

    Through our monthly logic calls, I don't ask for "content ideas." I ask for your convictions. I look for the stories you've told a hundred times in private meetings but never in public.

  • The Syntax Mirror

    I draft using a "Blind Review" process. I compare my drafts against your real-world voice notes to ensure the tone is indistinguishable from your organic thoughts.
